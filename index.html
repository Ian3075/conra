Fruit Catcher

This Unity project is a 3D arcade-style game where the player controls a character to catch falling fruits and avoid bombs. The player moves using the mouse, and their size can be adjusted via the mouse scroll wheel. Each fruit caught increases the score by 1 point, while each bomb hit reduces the player's lives by 1. The player starts with 3 lives, visually represented by hearts. When all lives are lost, a game over screen appears, displaying the player's final score and offering the option to restart the game. The project emphasizes player movement, object interaction, and a simple scoring and lives system.

Car Shooter

The Car Shooting Game is a thrilling 2D top-down arcade racer made in Unity that combines fast-paced racing with intense vehicular combat. Players control customizable cars, racing against AI opponents while utilizing weapons like missiles and lasers to eliminate rivals. The game features various vibrant tracks, retro pixel-art graphics, and engaging gameplay mechanics, including drifting and braking, to enhance the racing experience.

Adventure Time

Adventure Time is a 2D platformer game made in Unity. In this game, the player controls a character that embarks on an epic adventure through multiple levels. The objective is to guide the character through various obstacles, defeat enemies, collect valuable items, and reach the end goal to complete each level.

Obsidian Mirage

Obsidian Mirage is a unique and challenging 3D platformer game made in Unity in a group. Players take on the role of Y2, a robot on a singular mission: to move forward and collect coins for their people. The game's objective is simple yet deceptively difficult - navigate through intricate and surreal landscapes filled with obsidian platforms, obstacles, and collectible coins.
